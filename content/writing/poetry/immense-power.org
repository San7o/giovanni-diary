* Immense Power
:PROPERTIES:
:RSS: true
:DATE: 06 Sep 2025 00:00 GMT
:CATEGORY: Poetry
:AUTHOR: Giovanni Santini
:LINK: https://san7o.github.io/giovanni-diary/writing/poetry/immense-power.html

#+INDEX: Giovanni's Diary!Writing!Poetry!Immense power

#+begin_quote
A simple tool
with immense power

to do good
to make you happy
to hurt you
it must be used properly

yet why do I struggle
to rearrange the alphabet
#+end_quote
