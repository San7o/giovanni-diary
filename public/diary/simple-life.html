Life is simple - 16/03/2025

I was taking a wolk in my neighbourhood, there is not much too see here. A couple of small parks, some triees, and mostly residential houses. Yesterday I was watching a live of Prod. in real time, he was just answering some questions from chat. He is such an interesting and unique person, let me elaborate. Some people know him for his philosophy videos, I belive he has a formal education in philosophy and he often records his walks where he openly shared hit thoughts. He lives a simple life in the mountain, with little to no human contact or even electricity. Some other people know him for his emacs videos: he is a maintainer of some well-known pacakges and he is surely very knowledgable about the emacs-craft. He brings this two worlds togheter, that is why I think he is so unique. I started to watch his emacs videos and became interested into the philosophy ones. What strikes me is his self aweareness. Just by talking, he uncovered some of my own inner hazards and helped me get more aware of them. What I always do is to hide or suppress my emotional being, in order to live a more emotionless-life, a life where I can act objectively and with caution. I needed this because emotionally I am a very introverted, shy and insecure person. I always mind my own business and hardly interact with other people. However, If I rationalize my actions, I may come to the conclusion that talking to someone or starting a new activity is in my best interest, so I keep moving like this. The more I think of myself like this, the more my emotional-self hides and slowly dies. What I am realizing is that emotions are part of one's self and are essential to one's well being, what a breaking-news! The reasons why I hide are a bit more complicated that what I mentioned previously. Other than my insecurity, I have lived a past relationship with another person that I still cannot get over. I lived the relationship vividly, and I considered myself happy about this; but then It suddently come to a close, maybe after a lack of understanding of each other's emotions (I was still into her, but the feeling was not both-ways) so It came to an end quite badly and I am still recovering. One year and an half has passed but I still think about here, I cannot think about anyone else this way, but there is little to no-interest on the other side. I keep asking her how is she about every month, but I feel like I am the only one interested in the converstaion. Why am I still not over with this? I don't know, this is what my mind is doing, despite how much I try to suppress It, the feelings do not go away so quickly. When I have feelings, I feel them very stringly, I consider myself a very emotional person, that is why I try do suppress It as much as possible, because feelings are too strong. I want to try to allow myself this feelings, see If I get better.

Another point of reflection of this walk was history and searching through old stuff. In the park where I usually go there is a small wooden shelf under some stone structute. It is one of those shelfes where people may take or leave any book. I always stop there to see new books and If something interests me I would take It home for some time. Some of the books are narrative, some of them are didactical books, most of them are very old. What fascinates me is how those pieces of paper can open a window into the past and look at the world from a totally different prospective. For example, there was a local newspaper from a lot of time ago, It was one of the first with colored ink. It looked like from another era, totally another style. I imagined how people reading this newspaper looked like and I find It peaceful. I always imagine a simpler life, a lower life, and I enjoy those thoughts.

I am now letting my imagination fly and thinking about how my sourroundings looked like. I want to know the history of those places, if fills my mind. What I may do one day is to go to some little town and write some stories on what could have happened. Even better, I want to find historical books about those location, to trurly immerse myself in those times. One last think could be to describe some of the locations nowdays, so that people may know how life was here right now. I am not gonna lie, I am pretty excited about this.

Anyway, I am heading back home right now. I updated my todo list with some stuff.
