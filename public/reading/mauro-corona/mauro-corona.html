Mauro Corona

Mauro Corona is an Italian wood carver who lived much of his life within mountains and rural mountain villages. Unsurprisingly, he writes about that as well.

He has a deep love for mountain life, which fascinates me very much. His books reflect his raw experience in this world, nothing more than this but It is everything.

I am reading many of his books, some of my thoughts are accessible in the following documents:

  • La casa dei sette ponti
  • Lunario Sentimentale
  • Le voci del bosco
  • La via del sole
  • Vajont: Quelli del dopo
