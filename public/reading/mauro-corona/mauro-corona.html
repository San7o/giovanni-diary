Mauro Corona

Mauro Corona is an italian wood carver who lived much of his life within mountains and rulal mountain villages. Unsurprisingly, he writes about that aswell.

He has a deep love for mountain life, which fascinates me very much. His books reflect his raw experience in this world, nothing more than this but It is everything.
