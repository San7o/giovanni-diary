Volunteering

I volunteered on various associations for several years. This was part of my identity, I believed in the value of volunteering and in the associations I was participating with. Coincidentally, Trento was selected as the European Capital of Volunteering for 2024 so there was a lot of movement in this area.

I joined caritas in my home town, I was preparing dishes for the poor. In Trento I joined SAT where I was organizing Hikes with groups of kids, sometimes I did trail maintenance. I then joined FAI for some time to promote history. I guess contributing on openstreetmap is considered volunteering.

Unfortunately I had to stop to focus on my career, but once I will be more setup I am looking forward to do volunteering again.
